Mama's Best Pizza

Description

imagine the best pizza you have ever tasted, now prepare for that to be in second place.

Pepperoni, suasage, any meat'll do.

Ingredients

  • store made dough
  • your preferred meat based pizza topping
  • your preferred cheese
  • tomato sauce
  • mushrooms, if you're feeling froggy

Steps

  1. roll the dough into a circle
  2. sprinkle your favoirte cheese
  3. add your preferred toppings
  4. bake on high for as long as it takes
  5. ask your wife if you're doing this correctly
  6. let her tell you it's wrong
  7. she takes over and bakes it better than I could have
